How to use WELLlife Care
Open on YouTube ↗
1
Get Ready

Check the expiration date. Allow the test to reach room temperature. Wash hands before use. Open the package when ready to test.

2
Set up the Kit

Verify kit has a background card. Twist open the large cap and place the tube in the holder. Take the cassette out and lay it on a flat surface.

Reminder: Use test cassette within 1 hour of opening.

3
Open the Swab

Open the swab pouch carefully. Make sure not to touch the soft tip.

4
Swab Your Nose

Insert the swab 3/4 inch (1.5 cm) into right nostril, turn 5 times. Repeat in left nostril. Children under 14 should ask an adult.

Tip: Turn slowly and gently to prevent discomfort.

5
Mix the Sample

Place swab in tube, stir 10 circles gently. Wait 1 minute. Squeeze the tube to press the swab inside.

6
Dispose of the Swab

Remove the swab and throw it in the trash.

Reminder: Do not reuse any part of the test kit.

7
Prepare the Tube

Twist the large cap back on the tube, then open the small top cap.

8
Add Drops and Start Timer

Turn the tube upside down over the sample well. Add exactly 4 drops. Wait 10 minutes.

Tip: Do not read results before 10 or after 20 minutes.

9
Check for Control Line

Check for a C (control) line — if absent, the test is invalid, discard and retest.

C line + any F-A, F-B, or CoV line = positive. C line only = negative.
